About NYSL

Mission

To support young athletes lving in Chicago's northside neighbordhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn and practice skills related to the game of soccer, specifically those skills around team cooperation and good sportsmanship.

Vision

The youth side Young League aspires to develop strong, well-rounded and mindful athletes through the buildiing of character, self- discipline and leadership.

General Information

The Youth side soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago's northside neighborhoods an environment in wich to learn and play soccer to be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4-12 and reside in Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
